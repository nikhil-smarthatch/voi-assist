The Opportunity

Governments with large immigrant populations struggle with language barriers in citizen services. This results in high support costs and poor user experience.

We can build an AI-powered multilingual assistant that allows citizens to interact with government services in their own language through chat, voice, or kiosks.

This product can scale globally because many countries face the same problem. Government contracts for such platforms can reach millions of dollars, making it a strong opportunity for our company to move into AI-driven products instead of relying only on traditional development services.
